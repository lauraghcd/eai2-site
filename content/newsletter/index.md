## Current Publishing Model

EAI² keeps the content archive on this site first. Email subscription is currently handled as a lightweight invite list while we validate demand; RSS is available for readers who prefer not to provide an email address.

This keeps the first version simple:

- **EAI2.cloud** keeps the searchable content archive and canonical URLs
- **Email** is used only for readers who explicitly ask to receive updates
- **RSS** gives engineers and researchers a no-account subscription path
- **Paid subscription is not active**; RMB-friendly paid options should be evaluated separately before launch

## Editorial Bias

We care more about deployed robot-hours than demo polish.

That means every issue separates:

- official claims from verified deployment evidence
- funding momentum from real customer traction
- model benchmarks from robot performance
- hardware announcements from manufacturing reality

## Subscription Notes

Email updates are free during validation. We do not run a paid newsletter or self-hosted subscriber database yet. If a third-party subscription tool is added later, this page and the [Privacy Policy](/privacy/) will be updated before collecting subscribers through that tool.
