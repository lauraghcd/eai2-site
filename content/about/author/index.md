## Who We Are

**EAI² Editorial** is an independent team of technical writers, AI engineers, and robotics researchers focused on one mission: making the embodied AI landscape understandable for working engineers and researchers.

We don't represent a vendor, a venture fund, or a research lab. We have no products to sell you, no models to evangelize, and no horse in any race between humanoid manufacturers, foundation model providers, or simulation frameworks.

Our content reflects what we actually find when we do the work — reading the papers, comparing the models, deploying the policies, debugging the sim-to-real gap.

## Editorial Standards

**Sources:** Every claim links to its primary source — research papers, official documentation, company filings, or first-party benchmarks. If we can't source it, we don't publish it.

**Hands-on bias:** We prefer "we tried this" over "the paper says." Where possible, our tutorials are tested against real codebases (LeRobot, Isaac Lab, OpenVLA) and real hardware (when available).

**No-vendor-payola:** We accept no paid placements, no sponsored content, no affiliate steering. Affiliate links exist on this site (clearly disclosed in our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/)) but they don't influence what we write or recommend.

**Update cadence:** Articles are reviewed quarterly. Time-sensitive content (Funding Tracker, Industry Landscape) is updated monthly. The "Last updated" date on each article reflects real edits, not just frontmatter touches.

**AI-assisted, human-edited:** Content is drafted with AI assistance and edited, fact-checked, and signed off by human editors. We disclose this transparently because we believe it's the appropriate standard for 2026 technical publications.
