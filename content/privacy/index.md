## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **EAI² — Embodied AI Intelligence** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit `https://eai2.cloud` and `https://hub.eai2.cloud` (the "Site").

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we automatically receive:

- **Log data:** IP address, browser type, browser version, pages visited, time spent on pages, referring URLs
- **Cookies:** Used by analytics and advertising services (see below)
- **Device information:** Operating system, device type, screen size

### Information You Provide

We do not require account registration. The Site does not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., by contacting us via email).

## How We Use Information

- To analyze Site usage and improve content quality
- To serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
- To respond to inquiries you send us

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at:

- [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)
- [Network Advertising Initiative opt-out](https://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/)

For more information about Google's privacy practices, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand Site usage patterns. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. You can opt out via the [Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Affiliate Links

Some pages on this Site contain affiliate links. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps support the Site. We only recommend products and services we believe are valuable.

## Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect Site functionality.

## Data Retention

We retain log data for analytical purposes for up to 14 months. Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained longer.

## Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

The Site is hosted on Cloudflare's global network. Your data may be processed in countries other than your own.
