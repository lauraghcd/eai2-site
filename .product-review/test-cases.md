# Product Review Lifecycle Test Cases

## Case 1: New Engineer Evaluates Whether EAI2 Can Teach a Path

- Entry: Google search or homepage visit.
- Task: Understand whether the site offers a credible path from concepts to real robot deployment.
- Success: Finds Learning Path, sees ordered steps, opens at least two articles, and knows what to read next.
- Risk: Learning Path reads like notes rather than a course, causing low trust and high bounce.

## Case 2: Researcher Evaluates a Technical Comparison

- Entry: Search for VLA model, LeRobot, diffusion policy, or sim-to-real comparison.
- Task: Decide whether the article is deep and credible enough to cite or share.
- Success: Sees author/updated metadata, TL;DR, tables, sources, and related next reads.
- Risk: Thin pages, no author signal, or weak article-to-article context reduce citation trust.

## Case 3: Investor or Operator Tracks Industry Signals

- Entry: Search for humanoid funding, China humanoid companies, or robot market landscape.
- Task: Extract a reliable market map and return for updates.
- Success: Finds monthly tracker, related industry pieces, and a clear way to follow future updates.
- Risk: No update mechanism or trust framing means the user reads once and leaves.
