## Major Conclusion Audit

Issue type: prescriptive  
Issue: Should the next iteration prioritize AdSense approval mechanics or standalone product value?  
Conclusion: Standalone product value should be the priority; AdSense remains a downstream monetization channel.  
Reasons: Repo evidence showed trust signals had improved, while the user-visible subscription/content loop was still shallow. The site had a newsletter route but no real archive, no daily issue sample, and no regional strategy surface.  
Ambiguous terms: "value", "ready", "subscription".  
Value assumptions: readers care about durable signal extraction more than raw news flow; daily signals can improve return behavior.  
Descriptive assumptions: the NewsAgent daily format can be cleaned into publishable archive pages.  
Rival explanation: traffic may still depend mostly on search articles, not newsletter.  
Provisional verdict: keep as the current product direction, but validate with visits to `/newsletter/`, `/regions/`, RSS clicks, and email requests.

Issue type: descriptive  
Issue: Is the product now ready to validate a newsletter/daily-signal loop?  
Conclusion: It is ready for limited validation, not a full newsletter business.  
Reasons: The route, archive sample, subscription explanation, RSS path, and privacy note now exist. There is still no automated import pipeline, no subscriber database, and no delivery cadence.  
Missing information: actual subscriber requests, RSS usage, retention, and source quality over multiple imported issues.  
Provisional verdict: launchable for limited validation.

## Core Loop

Current intended loop:

1. Reader lands on article, homepage, newsletter, or regions page.
2. Reader understands EAI2 as a structured embodied AI field guide, not a generic blog.
3. Reader follows deeper links, RSS, or email subscription.
4. Returning reader uses daily signals and regional maps to decide what to read next.

Weakest step after this iteration: retention evidence. The site now has better return hooks, but there is no behavioral data yet.
